A Quick Biography of Me

I'm studying Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. Currently in my 2nd year.

I like thinking about stuff, in particular sciencey stuff. The absurdities of Quantum Physics intrigues me; I love pondering about the mystifying ideas that it presents especially when it can get spooky. I enjoy physics, but I have more of a love affair with computer science, which is why I chose computer science as my major in University. Being able to build something out of lines of text is a marvel to me and it probably always will be. Computer science is the outlet that allows me to showcase my logical problem solving skills.

Although at times when doing my computer sciency stuff, I think about how my life would be different if I was studying physics. How I would have a beard, eat physics people food, start my sentences with "According to the laws of phyiscs", and tell lame physics jokes that only my physics colleagues would understand. I'm 93.1% sure, physics majors are cooler than somputer science majors.However, all things considered, I am not that bummed out about not doing a major in phyiscs. Maybe I'll do a masters in physic.

At UW Aquaponics we build a super efficient ecosystem that produces food using fish poop and the nitrogen cycle. Aquaponics systems use almost 90% less water than a traditional agriculture system. The potential of Aquaponics is gigantic, and Aquaponics itself is in its enfancy stage. People around the world have large-scale aquaponics systems that grow We experiement and research with different agriculture variables (types of fish, water alkalinity) to get the best result
